Run in x86.

CONTROLS:
Tab		selects the model to move

Model Movements:
ENTER:		Turns the debug AABB's on and off.
SPACE:		Switches the physics libraries.

W		Moves the current model positive on the z axis. (backwards)
A		Moves the current model negativeon the x axis. (left)
S		Moves the current model negative on the z axis. (forward)
D		Moves the current model positive on the x axis. (right)

NumPad 1:	Moves the camera towards the current object.
Right Shift:	Moves the camera away from the current object.
Left		Moves the camera left.
Right		Moves the camera right.
